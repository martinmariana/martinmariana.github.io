Concerts

There’s different things about different concerts that make them great. The ability to hear the artist exactly as you would if they were next to you is special compared to listening to a studio recording. There’s no fine-tuning, after effects, or re-recordings. It’s just the artist speaking directly to you with both their lyrics and/or instrument. Also, it’s great to be in a space with many people that are passionate about the same music as you.
