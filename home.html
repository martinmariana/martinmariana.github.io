Home

Being at home is an escape, from all the places where you have to be a certain way. A refuge, from the things that you have to do to the things that you want to do. You come back home to that familiar place, where everything is right and every one is love. You enter your room for isolation, for rejuvenation, to restore what you have lost during the day in service to others and to yourself. Your comfort with your home is not squarely because it is a place, it is because everything that is important to you resides in it.
