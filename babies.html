Babies

Babies represent new hopes, new aspirations. They are brand new people - small things make them happy; if they love you, they love you with all their heart. Their smiles and hugs have a certain honesty that's difficult to find in grown-ups.Konrad Lorenz argued in 1949 that infantile features triggered nurturing responses in adults and that this was an evolutionary adaptation which helped ensure that adults cared for their children, ultimately securing the survival of the species. Some later scientific studies have provided further evidence for Lorenz's theory. For example, it has been shown that human adults react positively to infants who are stereotypically cute. Studies have also shown that responses to cuteness—and to facial attractiveness in general—seem to be similar across and within cultures.
